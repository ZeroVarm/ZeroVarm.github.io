About Us

Zerovarm provides a professional and discreet pest and vermin control service covering all of Northern Ireland.
We provide an agreed solution to your pest or vermin problem including Rats&Mice, Insects, and nuisance birds. We also specialise in agricultural and commercial pest and vermin control including Fox, Rabbits, Urban Gulls and Feral Pigeons.

Whether a domestic home, farm or commercial/business premises, if you have a pest or vermin problem then zerovarm can provide a range of effective solutions.

Contact us now to arrange a free first visit to discuss and advise on the service we can offer.
